Classic Beef Enchiladas are a saucy, cheesy and delicious recipe perfect for any occasion. Whether you need a quick meal during the weeknight or are serving up something special for the weekend, homemade beef enchiladas are perfect for both

>

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • large flour tortillas
  • 1 large can enchilada Sauce
  • 1 packet Taco seasoning
  • 1 1/2pound cheese shredded(i prefer cheddar or colbly jack)

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. Brown ground beef with taco seasoning
  3. drain meat fat (only required with higher fat ground beef
  4. Pour some Enchilada Sauce into 10x12 baking pan to cover bottom
  5. coat tortillas with Enchilada sauce
  6. add equal amount off beef and cheese(aprox.1/8th pound each)to tortilla and roll(done in the baking pan)
  7. repeat to fill pan(should have 8 Enchiladas)
  8. add any left over meat,sauce across the top
  9. cover with cheese
  10. bake 350 for 30-45 minuets or until cheese is melted
  11. let cool for 5 minuets or until sauce thickens
  12. top with sour cream sliced olives or guacamole to taste
